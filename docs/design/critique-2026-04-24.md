## Must-fix (blocking next UI build)

### 1. De-duplicate the outcome cards
Cliff-alert + safe-exit appear in both `CliffChart` and `LiveSummary`. Pick one home ‚Äî the brief puts them below the chart. Strip from `LiveSummary`; keep only the "today vs. after" delta + breakdown there.

### 2. Flatten cards-in-cards
Replace inner `rounded-xl` red-50/green-50 surfaces in `LiveSummary` and `CliffChart` with full-bleed horizontal bands + divider rules, or lift them out of the parent card into siblings. On the brief's hard "NEVER" list.

### 3. Restore mobile reading order
`LiveSummary` (or a slim strip variant) should sit between `ProfileForm` and `CliffChart` per brief Section 7. The chart is the payoff, not the lead-in to the summary.

---

## Recommendations (non-blocking polish)

### 4. ManagerBrief token drift
Change `bg-amber-50` ‚Üí `#FFF7ED` (orange-50); upgrade Copy CTA to `amber-500` and full-width on mobile. Matches brief Section 8 component spec.

### 5. Promote LiveSummary totals to Hero size
Bump from `text-3xl` to brief-spec `clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 3.5rem)` so they clearly out-weigh slider values. Resolves equal-weight squint failure.

### 6. Replace disabled State form row with a pill
Remove the Ohio-only disabled field from `ProfileForm`; show "Ohio" as a stone-100 pill under the h1 until more states ship. Fixes "Apology Empty State" feel, saves vertical space.

### 7. Dial down IncomeSlider value weight
Reduce slider `$X/yr` from `text-2xl font-extrabold` to a semibold mid-tier so it reads as an input echo, not a competing hero.

### 8. Create page-level design brief
`docs/design/cliffcheck-brief.md` ‚Äî capture attention hierarchy, hero definition, reading order. The design-critique skill expects this file; its absence was flagged as a gap.
